# Appendix C: AI Security Governance & Documentation

## Objective

This appendix outlines the fundamental requirements for setting up organizational structures, policies, and processes to manage AI security throughout the system lifecycle.

---

## AC.1 AI Risk Management Framework Adoption

Develop a formal framework to identify, assess, and mitigate AI-specific risks throughout the system lifecycle.

|   #    | Description                                                                                                      | Level | Role |
| :----: | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :---: | :--: |
| AC.1.1 | Verify that an AI-specific risk assessment methodology is documented and implemented.                            |   1   | D/V  |
| AC.1.2 | Confirm that risk assessments are performed at critical stages in the AI lifecycle and before any major changes. |   2   |  D   |
| AC.1.3 | Verify that the risk management framework aligns with established standards (e.g., NIST AI RMF).                 |   3   | D/V  |

---

## AC.2 AI Security Policy and Procedures

Establish and implement organizational standards for secure AI development, deployment, and operation.

|   #    | Description                                                                                                                | Level | Role |
| :----: | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :---: | :--: |
| AC.2.1 | Verify that documented AI security policies are in place.                                                                  |   1   | D/V  |
| AC.2.2 | Verify that policies are reviewed and updated at least annually and following significant changes in the threat landscape. |   2   |  D   |
| AC.2.3 | Verify that policies address all AISVS categories and applicable regulatory requirements.                                  |   3   | D/V  |
